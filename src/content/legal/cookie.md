## 1. What Are Cookies?

Cookies are small text files placed on your device when you visit a website. They are widely used to make websites work efficiently, remember your preferences, and provide information to site owners.

Similar technologies include:

- **Local storage / session storage** — browser-based storage for persisting data across sessions
- **Pixel tags / web beacons** — tiny images used to track page visits and email opens
- **Device fingerprinting** — collecting device attributes to identify returning visitors

## 2. Cookies We Use

### Strictly Necessary Cookies

These cookies are essential for the Services to function. They cannot be disabled.

| Cookie | Purpose | Duration |
| -------- | --------- | ---------- |
| `tz_session` | Maintains your login session | Session |
| `tz_csrf` | Protects against cross-site request forgery | Session |

### Performance & Analytics Cookies

These cookies help us understand how visitors interact with the Services so we can improve them. All data is aggregated and anonymised.

| Cookie | Provider | Purpose | Duration |
| -------- | ---------- | --------- | ---------- |
| `_ga` | Google Analytics | Distinguishes unique users | 2 years |

### Functional Cookies

These cookies enable enhanced functionality and personalisation.

| Cookie | Purpose | Duration |
| -------- | --------- | ---------- |
| `tz_prefs` | Stores your search and filter preferences | 6 months |
| `tz_recent` | Remembers recently viewed listings | 30 days |

We currently do not use marketing or advertising cookies. If this changes, we will update this policy and request your consent where required.

## 4. Your Cookie Choices

### Browser Settings

You can control cookies through your browser settings. Most browsers allow you to:

- View cookies stored on your device
- Delete all or specific cookies
- Block cookies from all or specific sites
- Set preferences for first-party vs third-party cookies

Note that disabling strictly necessary cookies may prevent the Services from functioning correctly.

### Opt-Out Links

- Google Analytics: [https://tools.google.com/dlpage/gaoptout](https://tools.google.com/dlpage/gaoptout)

### Do Not Track

Some browsers send a "Do Not Track" signal. We currently do not respond to DNT signals, but we respect your right to opt out of analytics tracking through the methods above.
